==============================================================================
				SPEEDSTER.NVIM
							      *speedster.nvim*

|speedster.nvim| helps you to warm up, practice and improve your typing speed.

==============================================================================

								       *run()*
|run()| runs the plugin.
>
  require("speedster").run()
<
Or, you can also use vim command in the command-mode
>
  :Speedster
<
